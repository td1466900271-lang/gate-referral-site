AI semis не заканчиваются, но рынок покупает certainty, not everything.

TSM и MU outperform NVDA, while crypto risk appetite softened. Это не broad liquidity melt-up, а structural AI semiconductor trade focused on HBM, advanced process, packaging and Asia manufacturing.

Главный вывод

TSM and MU strength vs NVDA weakness shows a more selective AI trade.

AI theme is not broken. Capital rotates away from the most crowded GPU leader into memory, foundry, advanced packaging and supply-chain bottlenecks with better earnings certainty.

1. TSM and MU are the signalTSM ADR +2.52% to $422.73 and MU +3.62% to $928.41, while NVDA fell about 0.91% to $212.90. This is internal AI semi rotation, not a broad index move.
2. Taiwan confirms the directionTaiwan Weighted near 44,641.39, up about 0.87%, while TSM 2330.TW near NT$2340, up about 1.74%, intraday high near NT$2360.
3. Crypto does not confirm full risk-onBTC near 74,313 and ETH near 2,019, both weaker versus prior references. AI semis are strong because of industry logic, not pure cross-asset euphoria.
4. Простыми словамиTSM still works as core AI infrastructure exposure. MU is high-beta profit-management exposure, with $900, $880 and $860 useful risk markers.

Список наблюдения

The next move depends on whether semiconductor breadth survives profit-taking.

  • Can TSM ADR hold $410-$405 support after moving above $422?
  • Can MU stay above $900, or does profit-taking start below $880?
  • Does NVDA weakness stay isolated, or drag SMH and SOXX lower?
  • Do BTC, ETH, funding and OI stabilize enough for HYPE and Hyperliquid sentiment?
  • Do AI capex, HBM pricing and CoWoS headlines keep supporting bottleneck trade?
Рамка действийHold core TSM, manage MU profits more actively, and avoid chasing after consecutive strong candles.

Most important market signal

TSM and MU strength with NVDA weakness does not look like AI exhaustion. It looks like rotation from crowded GPU leader into HBM, advanced process, CoWoS, packaging and Asia manufacturing certainty.

Institutional frame

BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan Asset Management and Morgan Stanley still frame AI as an infrastructure cycle across semiconductors, power, cooling and data centers. PIMCO, Gundlach and Dalio remind that high rates and leverage can cap valuations.

Positioning view

TSM remains suitable as core AI semiconductor exposure. MU is more cyclical and higher beta: after a strong move, $900, $880 and $860 are useful momentum and risk references.
